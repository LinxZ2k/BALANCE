The story of BALANCE

Our story begins with a group called "Four Charms" consisting of four teenage girls: Hsu Hsu Yati, Khaing Hnin San, Than Thar Su Nanda, and Eaint Phoo Nge. One day, while we were brainstorming about PBL projects, we weren't very productive. So, one of us said, "Why not make something about productivity and self-care? Clearly, we don't have either of those." And that was the origin of our website.

Our mission

So, what is our goal? Well, it's to encourage other students to be the best version of themselves. We know firsthand how annoying procrastination can be, so this website was created.
